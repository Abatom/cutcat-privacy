# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**CutCat**

Last updated / 最后更新: 2026-07-01

## English

CutCat is a video editing tool. All video processing is performed entirely on your device — your video and audio files are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with us or any third party.

**Video Data**: All editing, cutting, and exporting happens locally on your device. CutCat does not collect, store, upload, or share your videos, audio, or any file content.

**Advertising (Google Play version)**: The version of CutCat distributed on Google Play displays ads through Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve ads, AdMob collects and shares a device advertising identifier (Advertising ID / AAID) and related technical data. This data is used only for advertising and is transmitted over an encrypted connection. See Google's policy for how this data is handled: [https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) and [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Network Access**: The Google Play version requires a network connection solely to load ads. No video, audio, or file content is ever sent over the network. (Sideloaded builds that contain no ads do not use the network at all.)

**Consent (EEA/UK)**: Where required by law (e.g., GDPR), CutCat shows a consent form via Google's User Messaging Platform before requesting personalized ads. You can change your choice at any time through the app's consent settings.

**Permissions**: CutCat requests storage/media access solely to read and write video files on your device.

**Children**: CutCat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

**Your Rights**: You can reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), which limits ad personalization. For data handled by Google, see Google's privacy policy linked above.

**Third-Party Services**: The Google Play version integrates Google AdMob for advertising. No other analytics or tracking services are used.

**Contact**: If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues](https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues).

## 中文

CutCat 是一款视频剪辑工具，所有视频处理均在您的设备上完成——您的视频和音频文件绝不会被上传、传输或分享给我们或任何第三方。

**视频数据**：所有剪辑、切割和导出操作均在您的设备本地完成。CutCat 不会收集、存储、上传或分享您的视频、音频或任何文件内容。

**广告（Google Play 版本）**：通过 Google Play 分发的 CutCat 版本会使用 Google AdMob（Google 移动广告 SDK）展示广告。为投放广告，AdMob 会收集并分享设备广告标识符（广告 ID / AAID）及相关技术数据。这些数据仅用于广告用途，并通过加密连接传输。有关此数据的处理方式，请参阅 Google 的政策：[https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) 及 [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)。

**网络访问**：Google Play 版本仅为加载广告而需要网络连接。任何视频、音频或文件内容都绝不会通过网络发送。（不含广告的侧载版本完全不使用网络。）

**用户同意（欧洲经济区/英国）**：在法律要求的地区（如 GDPR），CutCat 会在请求个性化广告前，通过 Google 用户消息平台（UMP）向您展示同意表单。您可随时在应用的同意设置中更改您的选择。

**权限说明**：CutCat 仅请求存储/媒体权限，用于读取和写入您设备上的视频文件。

**儿童**：CutCat 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会在知情的情况下收集儿童数据。

**您的权利**：您可在设备设置中重置或删除广告 ID（设置 → Google → 广告），以限制广告个性化。对于 Google 处理的数据，请参阅上方链接的 Google 隐私政策。

**第三方服务**：Google Play 版本集成了 Google AdMob 用于广告。不使用任何其他分析或追踪服务。

**联系方式**：如对本隐私政策有疑问，请在 [https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues](https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues) 提交 issue。
