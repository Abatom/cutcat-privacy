# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**CutCat**

Last updated / 最后更新: 2026-05-30

## English

CutCat is a local video editing tool. All video processing is performed entirely on your device.

**Data Collection**: CutCat does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information.

**Network Access**: CutCat does not require or use any network connection. All operations are performed offline.

**Permissions**: CutCat requests storage access solely to read and write video files on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

**Third-Party Services**: CutCat does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

**Contact**: If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues](https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues).

---

## 中文

CutCat 是一款本地视频剪辑工具，所有视频处理均在您的设备上完成。

**数据收集**：CutCat 不会收集、存储、传输或分享任何个人数据或使用信息。

**网络访问**：CutCat 不需要也不使用任何网络连接，所有操作均离线完成。

**权限说明**：CutCat 仅请求存储权限用于读取和写入您设备上的视频文件，不会向外部服务器发送任何数据。

**第三方服务**：CutCat 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

**联系方式**：如对本隐私政策有疑问，请在 [https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues](https://github.com/Abatom/cutcat/issues) 提交 issue。
